1. Who We Are

PredictorArena ("we", "our", "us") is a free, skill-based sports prediction platform. We do not offer real-money gambling, betting services, or financial rewards. All predictions are community opinions for entertainment only.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use PredictorArena:

  • Username & Profile Data — The display name and optional profile photo you set.
  • Prediction & Game Data — Your prediction votes, scores, streaks, and leaderboard entries.
  • Device & Usage Data — Browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent. This is collected automatically via Google Analytics (if enabled) and Google AdSense.
  • Cookies — We use cookies and localStorage to save your preferences, game state, and session data. See Section 5 for details.

We do not collect your real name, phone number, email address, payment information, or any sensitive personal data.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

PredictorArena uses browser localStorage and cookies to:

  • Remember your username, profile, and prediction history.

  • Store your sport and theme preferences.

Third-party services (Google AdSense, Google Analytics) also set their own cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site features.

PredictorArena is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

PredictorArena is strictly a skill-based, free-to-play prediction game. No real money is wagered. No financial prizes are awarded. All predictions are user opinions shared for fun and community competition. This platform does not constitute gambling under any jurisdiction.
